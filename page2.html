That Day 💫

We met on Snapchat…

Within 5 minutes, I found out you lived nearby.
I was standing at a bus stop… we decided to meet.

It was hot… summer…
You in a black t-shirt, beige pants,
your face covered in sunlight.

Before I could see you properly… my bus came.
And I left.

But we kept talking…
I remember watching the sunset…
yet texting you felt more beautiful than it.
